Find energy-saving opportunities

The Energy Resource Hub connects homeowners, renters, and businesses in the City of Charlottesville and Albemarle County with government rebates and incentives. We find ways to lower the cost of heat pumps, insulation, electric appliances, and other energy efficiency upgrades. Get started by contacting our team or attending an upcoming event.

Incentives are complicated.
We are here to help.

The Inflation Reduction Act makes energy efficiency improvements more affordable. But finding the right rebate or tax credit for your specific situation is often a challenge. The Energy Resource Hub is here to help. Our support team works with you to understand your specific needs, find the right incentives, and help you apply. With our assistance, you won't miss an opportunity to save.
